Sweden is an EU member state with strong data protection traditions. Home to several major data centers and tech companies operating under EU law.

## Applicable Bloc Laws

These laws apply through Sweden's membership in regional agreements.

### GDPR (2018)

**General Data Protection Regulation**

Comprehensive data protection law giving individuals control over their personal data. Requires lawful basis for processing, data minimization, and grants rights including access, erasure, and portability.
