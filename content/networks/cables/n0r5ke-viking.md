## Overview

**N0r5ke Viking** is a domestic subsea cable system in Norway. Submarine Networks describes it as:

- **810 km** subsea cable between **Bergen** and **Trondheim**
- A **crosslink** between **Molde** and **Åndalsnes**
- **192-fibre-core** (96 fibre pairs)
- Operational since **December 2022**

## Why it matters

- Provides additional route diversity between western and central Norway
- Helps reduce dependency on single terrestrial corridors in some regions
