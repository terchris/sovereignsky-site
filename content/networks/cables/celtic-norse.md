## Overview

**Celtic Norse** is a planned subsea fibre system described as the first submarine fibre optic cable between **Norway and Ireland**, with onward connectivity toward the US via Irish landing ecosystems.

Submarine Networks describes:
- Route length **~2,000 km**
- Norway landing at **Øysanden** (Melhus municipality, south of Trondheim)
- Ireland landing at **Killala Bay** (County Mayo)
