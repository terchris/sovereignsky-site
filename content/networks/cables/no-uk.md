## Overview

The **NO-UK Cable System** is an open subsea cable system connecting **Stavanger (Norway)** with the **Newcastle area (United Kingdom)**. Submarine Networks describes the system as providing **eight dark fibre pairs** and up to **216 Tbps** system capacity, and notes provisional acceptance in **December 2021**.

## Why it matters for Norway

- Adds a direct UK corridor from south-west Norway
- Reduces reliance on routing via continental Europe for some traffic patterns
- Strengthens route diversity for Norwegian datacenters and carriers
