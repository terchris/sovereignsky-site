## Overview

**HAVSIL** is a repeaterless subsea fibre system connecting **Norway** to **Denmark**. Submarine Networks describes it as **120 km** long with **96 fibre pairs**, improving capacity and route diversity between Norway and continental Europe.

## Key facts (public)

| Attribute | Value |
|---|---|
| **Status** | ✅ Active |
| **Length (wet segment)** | 120 km |
| **Fibre pairs** | 96 |
| **Endpoints (marketed)** | N01 (Kristiansand) ↔ DK01 (Esbjerg) |
