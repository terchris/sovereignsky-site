## Overview

**Arctic Way** is a planned submarine cable system that will become the world's northernmost subsea cable. It will connect mainland Norway to **Jan Mayen** and **Svalbard**, replacing the aging Svalbard Fibre system and providing first-ever submarine fiber connectivity to Jan Mayen.

### Key Benefits

- **Svalbard**: Replaces aging Svalbard Fibre (2004) before end of service life
- **Jan Mayen**: First submarine fiber connection (currently satellite only)
- **Defense**: Supports Norwegian Armed Forces operations in the Arctic
- **Research**: Enables scientific activities and satellite operations
- **State Control**: Under Norwegian state ownership via Space Norway
